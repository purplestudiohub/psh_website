Our Work Culture

Creativity & Innovation

Employees are encouraged to think outside the box, experiment with new ideas, and push the boundaries of traditional marketing approaches. Creativity is valued, and innovative solutions are celebrated.

Collaboration & Teamwork

Given the interdisciplinary nature of social media and influencer marketing, teamwork and collaboration are essential. Our employees often work closely with colleagues from different departments, such as marketing, content creation, analytics, and client services, to deliver integrated campaigns and projects.

Entrepreneurial Spirit

Our company has a startup-like culture, where employees are encouraged to take initiative, show ownership, and think like entrepreneurs. This often involves a high level of autonomy, responsibility, and a willingness to take calculated risks.

Continous Learning & Development

Given the fast-paced nature of the industry, continuous learning and professional development are emphasized. We offer trainings programs, workshops, conferences, and other opportunities for employees to enhance their skills, stay updated on industry trends, and advance their careers.

Diversity & Inclusion

Diversity and inclusion are valued in this workplace, as diverse perspectives and backgrounds contribute to more innovative and effective solutions. Our company prioritizes creating an inclusive environment where all employees feel respected, heard, and valued.

Work -Life Balance

While the industry may involve occasional periods of high intensity and tight deadlines, our company often strive to promote a healthy work-life balance. This may include flexible work hours, remote work options, and initiatives to support employee well-being and mental health.
